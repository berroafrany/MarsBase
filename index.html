Mars Base - Daily Report

Mars Mission Log — Sol 17

Commander: Dr. Elena Serrano

Location: Ares Base, Valles Marineris Sector

Daily Summary:

Today was a maintenance-focused day. We conducted a full diagnostic on Habitat Module 3’s oxygen recycling unit. Minor fluctuations in pressure were traced to a partially frozen valve, which we replaced with a spare. EVA team completed terrain mapping on Grid 12B, noting a high concentration of hematite — potential for future resource extraction.

Notable Events:

Solar flare warning prompted a 90-minute shelter protocol. All systems held stable.

3D printer successfully produced replacement hand tools using regolith-infused filament — first test of local material integration. Received delayed transmission from Earth: morale boost for the crew with personal messages.
